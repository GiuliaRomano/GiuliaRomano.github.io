I am a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the Department of Electronics, Information and Bioengineering (DEIB) at Politecnico di Milano, where I am advised by Professor Nicola Gatti.

My work focuses on problems at the intersection of Algorithmic Game Theory, Mechanism Design and Online Learning. In particular, I am interested in designing efficient algorithms and auction mechanisms for scenarios in which strategic agents aim at selling and/or advertising products. I am also interested in multi-armed bandits problems and optimization under uncertainty.
